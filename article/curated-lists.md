# The 2026 World Cup — The Robust Watch List

> Working reference for Part 3. Six categories, the names that matter, and a one-line "why." Built from the Guardian player guide + the squad data in this repo. Blurbs are deliberately short so they're easy to lift into the article or the site.

Tournament: FIFA World Cup 2026 · 48 teams · Hosts: USA, Canada, Mexico · Kickoff June 11 · Final July 19, MetLife Stadium.

See `data/players.csv` (the `badges` column) or the interactive site for all 48.
